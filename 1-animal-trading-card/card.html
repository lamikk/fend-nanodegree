Lemur of Madagascar

He likes to move it move it!

  • Family: Primates
  • Scientific Name: Lemur Catta
  • Avg Size: 40 cm.
  • Habitat: Madagascar

Lemurs name comes from a latin word meaning "spirit od the night" due to their ghostly look.
They live in huge packs where hyerarchy is strictly respected, and those with the smelliest tails are considered alpha males.
They usually have good relationship with humans.
